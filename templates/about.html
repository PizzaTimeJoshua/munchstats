This site was the product of "Pikalytics is taking a while to update its usage stats." So instead of waiting, I made this site.

FAQ

Where does the data come from?

It comes from Pokemon Showdown's Monthly Usage Stats, the same place as Pikalytics!

How often does this get updated?

Once a month. Near the beginning of the month when Showdown releases it's usage stats.

Pokemon Showdown doesn't provide Tera Data, where do you get it from?

Starting September 1st 2024, Pokemon Showdown DOES show Tera Data. Previously, this used to be based off public replay data.
